# Contributing Factors Analysis

This folder explores the main factors responsible for motor vehicle crashes in New York City.  
The goal was to identify the most common causes of accidents and how they differ across boroughs.

## üó∫Ô∏è Top Contributing Factors by Borough
![Top Contributing Factors by Borough](./Top%20Contributing%20Factors%20by%20Borough.png)

**Insights:**
- **Brooklyn** and **Queens** report the highest crash numbers across most contributing factors.  
- **Driver Distraction** and **Unspecified causes** dominate across all boroughs.  
- **Failure to Yield** is more frequent in **Queens and Manhattan**, likely due to dense intersections.  
- **Backing Unsafely** occurs more often in **Brooklyn**, possibly due to high parking density.  
- Smaller boroughs like **Staten Island** have fewer crashes overall.

### üß≠ Summary
- **Driver distraction** remains the leading cause of crashes citywide.  
- Reporting gaps (the ‚ÄúUnspecified‚Äù category) limit the accuracy of root-cause analysis.
